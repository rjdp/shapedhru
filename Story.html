Empowerment of women in the country is a process that has long been underway and the results are starting to show. The enrollment figures for women in schools, colleges and other professional institutes have showed an improvement with more women being involved in both technical and traditional job sectors. Women self-help groups have been setup in the rural districts to fight for the right of women in India and provide a source of empowerment for them through various means. The proposal for the passing of bills and laws in the Parliament towards protection of women against sexual assaults and violence will be a major step forward towards setting up of women's rights in India. As and when the thirty-three per cent reservation of seat for women in the Parliament gets completed, women of the nation will find a much louder voice for themselves in the higher seats of power in the Indian democracy.
